# Product Requirements Document

# Product Name: OnlineSkiller

## 1. Product Tagline

OnlineSkiller
Launch your online digital product, business, and get marketing strategies to sell it.

## 2. Product Overview

OnlineSkiller is a subscription-based SaaS platform that helps creators, coaches, freelancers, tutors, consultants, and small digital entrepreneurs launch their online business without needing to code.

Users can sign up, create a digital product or business page, choose a professional template, add their information, embed videos, upload images, add pricing, connect call-to-action links, preview the website, and publish it after paying a monthly subscription.

OnlineSkiller also gives users a marketing section where they can access ready-to-use promotional videos, captions, hooks, hashtags, sales scripts, content calendars, and marketing strategies to help them sell their product or service online.

The platform does not take commission from the user’s sales in the first version. It earns money through monthly subscription plans.

## 8. Business Model

The user pays to keep their page live and access marketing resources.

The platform does not take commission from their earnings in version one.

## 27. Access Control Rules

Creators can only access their own:

Profile
Pages
Videos
Lessons
Billing
Saved marketing assets
Saved marketing strategies
Calendar items
Integrations

Admins can access all platform data.

Public visitors can only access live pages connected to an active subscription.

Dashboard routes must be protected by Clerk.

Server-side checks must always confirm ownership.

## 28. Subscription Gate

When a creator clicks publish:

Check if creator has an active or trialing subscription.

If yes:

Set page status to live
Set is_live to true
Set published_at to current date

If no:

Redirect to billing page
Show message: “Choose a plan to make your page live.”

If a subscription expires:

Keep the creator dashboard accessible
Keep editing and preview available
Disable public page access
Show unavailable page to public visitors
